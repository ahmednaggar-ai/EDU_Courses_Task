Privacy Policy

How EduFlow Admin collects, uses, and protects institutional data.

EduFlow Systems Inc. is committed to protecting the privacy of students, faculty, and administrative users. This policy describes how institutional data is handled within the EduFlow Admin platform.

Data Collection

We collect account information, course records, enrollment data, and usage analytics necessary to operate the administrative dashboard.

Data Security

All institutional data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Access is restricted by role-based permissions and audited for compliance.

Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact privacy@eduflow.edu.
